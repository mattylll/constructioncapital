Development Finance

Senior and mezzanine debt for your development projects

What We Provide

We arrange development finance for residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects across the UK. Whether you need senior debt, mezzanine finance, or a combination of both, we structure funding packages that align with your project timeline and cashflow requirements.

Project Types We Finance:

  • Residential developments (houses and apartments)
  • Commercial developments (offices, retail, industrial)
  • Mixed-use schemes
  • Student accommodation
  • Build-to-rent projects
  • Conversions and refurbishments

Typical Terms

  • Loan Size: £500k to £50m+
  • LTC: Up to 70% (senior debt)
  • LTGDV: Up to 60-65%
  • Term: 12-36 months
  • Interest: Variable or fixed rates
  • Arrangement Fees: Typically 1-2%

Note: Terms vary by lender, project type, and borrower experience

Equity Funding

Access to institutional investors, family offices, and JV partners

Our Equity Sources

We maintain active relationships with over 10 equity providers actively seeking development opportunities. Whether you need a capital partner for a single project or are looking for a long-term joint venture relationship, we can make the right introduction.

Equity Providers Include:

  • Family offices
  • Institutional funds
  • High-net-worth individuals
  • Property companies seeking JV opportunities
  • Developer/investor hybrids

Typical Equity Structures

  • Equity Investment: £250k to £20m+
  • Profit Share: Typically 40-60% to equity partner
  • Hurdle Rates: Often structured with preferred returns
  • Control: Varies from passive to active involvement
  • Exit: Usually at project completion/sale

Structures are highly negotiable and project-specific

Refinancing

Optimize your existing facilities or release capital for your next project

When to Refinance

Refinancing can help you reduce costs, extend terms, release capital, or move to a more suitable lender. We review your current arrangements and source competitive alternatives.

Common Refinancing Scenarios:

  • Reducing interest costs on existing facilities
  • Extending loan terms to match project timelines
  • Releasing equity from completed/stabilized projects
  • Consolidating multiple facilities
  • Moving from construction to term finance
  • Exiting expensive mezzanine finance early

Refinancing Benefits

  • Lower interest rates and fees
  • Extended loan periods
  • Capital release for new projects
  • Better lender relationships
  • Improved loan terms and covenants
  • Consolidated borrowing facilities

What We Need

  • Details of existing facility
  • Current project status
  • Your refinancing objectives
  • Recent valuation (if available)

Site Acquisition Finance

Bridge funding for land purchases and pre-planning development

Fast Capital for Opportunities

When you find the right development site, speed is critical. We provide fast-turnaround bridge finance to help you secure land acquisitions before moving to development finance once planning is in place.

Use Cases:

  • Land purchase with planning application to follow
  • Sites with existing planning (bridge to development finance)
  • Auction purchases requiring quick completion
  • Off-market opportunities with tight timescales
  • Option agreements requiring funding

Site Acquisition Terms

  • Loan Size: £250k to £10m+
  • LTV: Typically up to 70%
  • Term: 3-24 months
  • Speed: Completions from 2 weeks
  • Exit: Refinance to development finance or sale

Our Process

  1. Initial assessment within 24 hours
  2. Terms agreed in principle within days
  3. Valuation and legal work progressed urgently
  4. Completion typically 2-4 weeks

Joint Ventures

Structuring and placement for development partnerships

Partnership Opportunities

Joint ventures allow developers to access larger projects, share risk, and leverage complementary skills and resources. We facilitate JV partnerships and help structure mutually beneficial arrangements.

JV Scenarios We Facilitate:

  • Developer + Landowner: Unlocking development value from land
  • Developer + Capital Partner: Scaling projects with equity backing
  • Developer + Developer: Combining expertise and resources
  • Developer + Institutional Partner: Access to large-scale opportunities
  • Multi-party JVs: Complex structures for major projects

What We Do

  • Identify suitable JV partners
  • Structure profit share and control arrangements
  • Negotiate heads of terms
  • Coordinate legal documentation
  • Arrange debt finance for the JV
  • Advise on governance and decision-making

Key JV Considerations

  • Profit share and waterfall structures
  • Decision-making and control
  • Capital contributions and timing
  • Exit mechanisms
  • Dispute resolution
  • Partner alignment and objectives
