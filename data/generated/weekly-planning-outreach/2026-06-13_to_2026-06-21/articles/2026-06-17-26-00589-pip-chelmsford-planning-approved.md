## Chelmsford planning approval

Chelmsford City Council has approved planning application 26/00589/PIP for 4 residential units at Land At Margaretting Service Station Main Road Margaretting Ingatestone Essex. The approved proposal is: Permission In Principle To Establish The Principle Of Residential Development For Four Dwellings

The application was decided on 17/06/2026. Construction Capital has reviewed the local market data for Chelmsford, Essex to provide a high-level funding view for the site.
